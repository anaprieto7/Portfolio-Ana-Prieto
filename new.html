About me

I'm Ana Prieto, a UX/UI designer and digital product generalist. I'm fascinated by how design, information architecture and technology come together to make complex systems usable and trustworthy.

Over the last years, I've been working mainly in the logistics and e-commerce tech space: returns management platforms, warehouse dashboards and handheld tools for operators. I like working where user flows, data and implementation meet.

I'm currently focusing on building experiences that can scale and evolve, including products that integrate AI while still feeling clear, efficient and human.
